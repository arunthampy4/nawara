Which areas in Muscat do you cover?

We serve homes and businesses across all of Muscat and the wider Oman region — including Al Khuwair, Ruwi, Al Mawaleh, Seeb, Bawshar and more.

Are your products safe for children and pets?

Yes. We use non-toxic, eco-friendly products and German-recommended equipment that are safe around children, pets and all surface types.

Do you offer one-off jobs or only contracts?

Both. You can book a single one-off job or set up a recurring daily, weekly or monthly schedule — whatever suits you.

How do I get a price?

Call or WhatsApp us, or send the quick quote form. For larger jobs we'll do a short survey and give you a clear, fixed price with no surprises.

Is the pest control treatment odorless and safe to stay during?

Our standard insect treatments are odorless and hassle-free, so in most cases there's no need to leave the premises. We'll always advise you per job.
